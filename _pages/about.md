# About Me

I am Jun Zhang, Ph.D., Professor at **Fudan University**, Shanghai, China. My research focuses on the intersection of mathematics, psychology, and cognitive science, with particular interests in:

- **Information Geometry** and its applications to machine learning and statistics
- **Mathematical Psychology** and decision making
- **Cognitive Science** and theory of mind
- **Visual Perception** and sensory processing
